# Product Vision

## Project Overview

The Wishes is a collaborative platform designed to help people preserve meaningful gift ideas throughout the year, making gift-giving more personal, thoughtful, and effortless.

Instead of relying on memory or asking "What do you want?" before every special occasion, users can naturally collect and organize their wishes over time. Friends, family members, and partners can access these wishlists when appropriate, allowing them to choose meaningful gifts while preserving the surprise.

The Wishes is not built around products or shopping. It is built around people, relationships, and the small moments that make thoughtful gifts memorable.

---

## Problem Statement

Gift-giving is often spontaneous and stressful.

People usually discover things they would love to receive throughout the year, but those ideas are quickly forgotten or scattered across notes, screenshots, saved links, messages, and social media.

When birthdays, anniversaries, holidays, or other celebrations arrive, family members and friends frequently have to ask what someone wants, reducing the surprise and often leading to rushed or less meaningful gifts.

There is currently no centralized and personal space specifically designed to capture, organize, and share wishes in a way that strengthens relationships rather than focusing on shopping.

---

## Target Audience

The Wishes is designed for anyone who regularly exchanges gifts with people they care about.

This includes:

- Couples
- Families
- Friends
- Classmates
- Coworkers
- Communities

The platform is intended for users who value thoughtful gifts, meaningful relationships, and long-term organization over last-minute purchases.

---

## Vision

Our vision is to become the place where people naturally save the little things that make them happy, creating stronger relationships through more meaningful gift-giving.

The Wishes aims to become part of everyday life rather than an application used only during birthdays or holidays.

---

## Mission

To help people remember, organize, and share their wishes in a simple and personal way, allowing others to give gifts that truly matter.

---

## Core Values

- People before products.
- Relationships before transactions.
- Simplicity over complexity.
- Flexibility over rigid structures.
- Privacy by choice.
- Trust through transparency.
- Preserve the surprise.
- Make thoughtful gift-giving accessible to everyone.

---

## Goals

The primary goals of The Wishes are:

- Eliminate last-minute gift decisions.
- Preserve gift ideas throughout the year.
- Make thoughtful gifts easier to give.
- Strengthen personal relationships.
- Create a pleasant and intuitive user experience.
- Support different kinds of relationships through shared or private wishlists.
- Allow users to organize wishes in a flexible and personalized way.

---

## Non-Goals

The Wishes is not intended to become:

- An online marketplace.
- An e-commerce platform.
- A social media network.
- A product catalog.
- A price comparison website.
- A mandatory shopping platform.

Purchasing items is never the primary objective of the application.

---

## Unique Value Proposition

Unlike traditional wishlists, The Wishes focuses on people rather than products.

Instead of encouraging purchases, it encourages users to remember meaningful wishes, preserve surprises, and create stronger emotional connections through thoughtful gift-giving.

The platform adapts to each user's wishes instead of forcing wishes into predefined shopping formats.

---

## Design Principles

The product should always feel:

- Warm
- Friendly
- Trustworthy
- Personal
- Intuitive
- Flexible
- Calm

Users should be able to accomplish their goals with little or no learning curve.

---

## Success Metrics

The product will be considered successful if users:

- Use it throughout the year instead of only during holidays.
- Build long-term wishlists.
- Create groups with family and friends.
- Return frequently to update their wishes.
- Feel more confident when choosing gifts.
- Report having fewer last-minute gift decisions.

---

## Future Vision

The Wishes has the potential to evolve into a complete relationship-centered platform where people can preserve meaningful wishes, celebrate important moments, and create stronger emotional connections with those they care about.

Every future feature should reinforce the core purpose of the platform:

Helping people give better gifts by understanding each other better.
